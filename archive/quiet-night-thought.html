《未选择的路》

黄huáng 色sè 的de 树shù 林lín 里lǐ 分fēn 出chū 两liǎng 条tiáo 路lù，
可kě 惜xī 我wǒ 不bù 能néng 同tóng 时shí 去qù 涉shè 足zú，
我wǒ 在zài 那nà 路lù 口kǒu 久jiǔ 久jiǔ 伫zhù 立lì，
我wǒ 向xiàng 着zhe 一yí 条tiáo 路lù 极jí 目mù 望wàng 去qù，
直zhí 到dào 它tā 消xiāo 失shī 在zài 丛cóng 林lín 深shēn 处chù。

但dàn 我wǒ 却què 选xuǎn 了le 另lìng 外wài 一yī 条tiáo 路lù，
它tā 荒huāng 草cǎo 萋qī 萋qī， 十shí 分fēn 幽yōu 寂jì，
显xiǎn 得de 更gèng 诱yòu 人rén， 更gèng 美měi 丽lì；
虽suī 然rán 在zài 这zhè 两liǎng 条tiáo 小xiǎo 路lù 上shàng，
却què 很hěn 少shǎo 留liú 下xià 旅lǚ 人rén 的de 足zú 迹jì。

那nà 天tiān 清qīng 晨chén 落luò 叶yè 满mǎn 地dì，
两liǎng 条tiáo 路lù 都dōu 未wèi 经jīng 脚jiǎo 印yìn 污wū 染rǎn。
啊ā， 留liú 下xià 一yī 条tiáo 路lù 等děng 改gǎi 日rì 再zài 见jiàn!
但dàn 我wǒ 知zhī 道dào 路lù 径jìng 延yán 绵mián 无wú 尽jìn 头tóu，
恐kǒng 怕pà 我wǒ 难nán 以yǐ 再zài 回huí 返fǎn。

也yě 许xǔ 多duō 少shǎo 年nián 后hòu 在zài 某mǒu 个gè 地dì 方fāng，
我wǒ 将jiāng 轻qīng 声shēng 叹tàn 息xī 将jiāng 往wǎng 事shì 回huí 顾gù：
一yí 片piàn 树shù 林lín 里lǐ 分fēn 出chū 两liǎng 条tiáo 路lù ──
而ér 我wǒ 选xuǎn 择zé 了le 人rén 迹jì 更gèng 少shǎo 的de 一yī 条tiáo，
从cóng 此cǐ 决jué 定dìng 了le 我wǒ 一yī 生shēng 的de 道dào 路lù。

The Road Not Yet Chosen

In the yellow forest, two roads separate,
It's a pity that I can't step foot on [them] at the same time,
At that intersection I stand for a long time,
I look toward one road as far as I can see,
Until it vanishes into the depths of the jungle.

But I chose the other road,
Its weeds lush, very quiet,
[It] seems more alluring, more beautiful;
Although on these two roads,
Few remained of travelers' footprints.

Early that morning, fallen leaves covering the ground,
The two roads both not yet polluted by footprints.
Ah, [I will] meet the remaining road again some other day!
But I know the path stretches continuously without end,
I'm afraid I find it difficult to return again.

Perhaps many years later at a certain place,
I will quietly sigh, looking back on past events:
In a part of the forest, two roads separate—
And I chose the road with fewer footprints,
From this point on, I decided the road of my life.
